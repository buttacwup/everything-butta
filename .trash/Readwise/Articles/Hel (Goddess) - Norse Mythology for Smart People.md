# Hel (Goddess) - Norse Mythology for Smart People

![rw-book-cover](https://readwise-assets.s3.amazonaws.com/static/images/article2.74d541386bbf.png)

## Highlights
- Hel (Old Norse Hel, “Hidden”[1]) is a giantess and/or goddess who rules over the identically-named Hel, the underworld
- Hel is the daughter of Loki and the giantess Angrboda
- the sister of the wolf Fenrir and the world serpent, Jormungand.
- Hel is generally presented as being rather greedy, harsh, and cruel, or at least indifferent to the concerns of both the living and the dead
- her appearance as being half-black, half-white, and with a perpetually grim and fierce expression on her face
